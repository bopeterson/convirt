Virtual Conferencing and Social VR

Virtual Reality as a medium for scholarly and social interaction
In conjunction with the conference on the Digital Reading Condition at Aarhus University (February 11, 2021), we have created a suite of VR rooms for attendees and guests.

All these rooms are identical, but occupancy is limited to 25. Join any one by clicking on the button. If that room is full, please try the next. (Or choose the surprise, which could be any of the four.)

After your visit
We are researching the potential role of Virtual Reality environments for scholarly exchange, and we are asking you to answer a few questions after your experience in our Hubs room. The survey is anonymous and the data that we collect will be used to inform our iterative design of the Hubs environment as well as for future research writing.
Take the survey
Virtual Conferencing is a pilot project funded by Malmö University. The full project title is: Virtual conferencing to promote research and scholarly exchange during the current pandemic and possible future disruptions. The project is part of Malmö University's research program Data Society.
